About Us

Petsoul is a non profit working tirlessly to make sure all pets have a loving home. We are a group of volunteers who are passionate about animals and we are dedicated to saving the lives of pets in kenya

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a world where every pet has a loving and caring home. We strive to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome pets in need, ensuring they receive the medical care and attention they deserve
